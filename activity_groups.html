SWAKCHHATA & GREEN INITIATIVE

The ecosystem in which we live provides natural resources for humans that are essential to our health, quality of life and survival. The most popular resources of this ecosystem are Soil, Water Timber, and Wildlife. With more and more urbanisation and industrialisation there is massive depletion of these resources.
Society members strive to bring a visible change and improve our ecosystem by indulging in activities like:

  • Tree Plantation
  • Locality Clean Up Drive
  • Rain Water Harvesting
  • Exploring recycling/reusing efforts
  • Creating social awareness for environmental improvement initiatives.

SOCIETY WELFARE & SOCIAL WELL BEING

With Self Compassion and empathetic mind set & philanthropic motives of group members who poised to contribute to the well-being of the community and the Society as whole, formed this section of the Society with single idea and motive that is, to reach out to the needy ones and enable them to have their minimum need fulfilled and to make a healthy environment for growth. This section of Hamara Sankalp Society has mandate to focus of Social welfare and Community wellbeing with following functions/Activities:

  • Organising Free Health checkup for needy ones.
  • Organising Enabling Activities at the locality which has been ignored by govt and/or other agencies.
  • Distribution of Books and necessary articles to poor students.
  • Programs to be organised for helpless old citizens.
  • Financial assistance to needy and poor people.
  • Skill Development.
  • Helping disable people.
  • Other activities to social welfare.
