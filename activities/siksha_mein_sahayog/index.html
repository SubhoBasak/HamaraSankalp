Siksha mein Sahayog

Assistance in Education for needy bright Students

It is felt by the Society members that there are many bright students with ﬁnancial hardship of their family. The society members decided to help those students between Class one and Class ten who are good in study but they need some kind of ﬁnancial help to continue their studies. In this project the society adopted education of a girl student In Durgapur in the month of October 2020.

Society has set a target of help minimum 100 students by August 2021.
