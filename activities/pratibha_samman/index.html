PRATIBHA SAMMAN

Facilitation of Class X - Toppers of all the schools of Burnpur

On 15th August 2020, On the occasion of the 74th Independence Day PARTIBHA SAMMAN activity was organized by EDUCATION & Cultutal Activity Group of HAMARA SANKALP to felicitate class X toppers of all schools of Burnpur of West Bengal Board and CBSE Board.
Two falicitation program wre organised at Burnpur Boy’s High School for the toppers of BBHS (HS) and Burnpur Girl’s School and at Burnpur Adarsh Vidayalya for Class X toppers of Burnpur Adarsh vidyalya (Boys and Girls). For School Toppers of other Schools like Burnpur Riverside School, Gurunanak Girls school, Subhash Pally School, Gandhi Vidyalaya, Bari vidyalya, Bina Pani School, Bari Vidyalya , Rahamat Nagar school etc , Hamara Sankalp adopted a new way for award distribution in the light of the COVID19 Pandemic situation. Volunteers contacted all schools and parents of all toppers and noted thier address and on 15th August , Volenteers went to the residance of them and the momento was given to the toppers by thier mother and certificate and a book written by Bhatan Ratna Late APJ Kalam Sahab were handed over to them.
Shri Rajesh Deogharia, Shri Ajay Verma, Shri Balwant Singh, Smt Munmun Rai, Shri Sajal Maji, Smt Pinky Singh, Md. Aslam, Shri Malay Mazumdar, Shri Ajay Singh ( teacher), Smt. Sangita Mitra, shri Anil Kumar Singh, Shri Bikram Paswan, Shri Umesh prasad, Shri Surjo Sarathi Dhar, Shri Ishwar Dayal Singh, Shri Paratyush Kumar and Shri Munna Yadav contributed financially and Shri Kamelendu Mishra, arranged all Books Written by Bhatan Ratna Late APJ Kalam Sahab.
