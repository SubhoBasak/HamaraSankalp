Helping Hands during LOCK DOWN

Distribution of Essential items to needy people. During may and June 2020.

During the lockdown one and two when there was a great fear of CORONA pandemic and all most all people were locked them selves in their home, People became job less, No food was available to poor people, Hamara Sankalp society took the responsibility to help poor people and during May and June 2020 over 30 camps were organised to distributed Food and other essential times to needy people.
Durig this period Essential items were distributed to over 1500 needy people in Burnpur and Durgapur. Shri Rajesh Deogharia Deogharia, Shri Bikram, Shri Umesh Prasad, Shri Sujoy Rohut, Smt Munmun Rai, Shri Suroj Sarathi Dhar, Shri Balwant Sing, Shri Arun Pandit etc played very crucial role in this activity. Over 60 Members from Burnpur, Asansol and Drugapur contributed for this cause.

I Plant...I Care...It's My New Family

In order to senitie the impotrance of plants and trees in our life, GREEN INITIATIVE Group of HAMARA SANKALP organised I Plant....I Care...It's My New Family activity on 3rd August 2020 on the Occasion of Raksha Bandhan. Over 350 people across in 3 countried and 22 cities of India planted saplings individually, tied RAKSHA SUTRA to the plant and took pledge to safe guard environment, to minimize use of one time usable plastic articles, to Reduce, to Reuse and to Recycle in daily life. Cities where the activity taken place Asansol, Burnpur, Australia, Bakura, Bangalore, Canada, Chandan Nagar, Delhi, Deoria, Dhanbad, Durgapur, Gorakhpur, Jabalpur, Kodarma, Kolkata Lucknow, Motihari, Mumbai, Patna, Pune, Varanasi, Siwan, Bankura. The Society has taken a target of 100 cities across 10 contries for next year.

My birthday - My Plant

Green Initiative Group of Hamara Sankalp celebrates society memebers'Birthday in unique style. In the Morning the Committee members visit the residence of the member on his/her Birthday and wish him/her Happy Birthday with Flowers. Later He/She is taken to the place of Plantation and a plant is planted by him/her and the Environment pledge is taken by Him/her with a promise to safe guide the plant for a year till next birthday. In the Evening a Photo Frame Momento is given to the member as His/Her Birthday Gift. This program is being coordinate by Shri Ajay Verma and Shri Umesh Prasad.
