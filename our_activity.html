Helping Hands during LOCK DOWN

Distribution of Essential items to needy people. During may and June 2020.

During the lockdown one and two when there was a great fear of CORONA pandemic and all most all people were locked them selves in their home, People became job less , No food was available to poor people , Hamara Sankalp society took the responsibility to help poor people and during May and June 2020 over 30 camps were organised to distribute Food and other essential items to needy people .
During this period Essential items were distributed to over 1500 needy people in Burnpur and Durgapur. Shri Rajesh Deogharia Deogharia, Shri Bikram, Shri Umesh Prasad, Shri Sujoy Rohut, Smt Munmun Rai, Shri Surjo Sarathi Dhar, Shri Balwant singh, Shri Arun Pandit etc played very crucial role in this activity. over 60 Members from Burnpur, Asansol and Durgapur contributed for this noble cause .

I Plant...I Care...It's My New Family

In order to sensitize the impotrance of plants and trees in our life, GREEN INITIATIVE Group of HAMARA SANKALP organised I Plant… I Care… It’s My New Family activity on 3rd August 2020 on the Occasion of Raksha Bandhan. Over 350 people across in 3 countries and 22 cities of India planted saplings individually, tied RAKSHA SUTRA to the palnt and took pledge to safe guard environment, to minimize use of one time usable plastic articles, to Reduce, to Reuse and to Recycle in daily life. Cities where the activity taken place Asansol, Burnpur, Australia, Bakura, Bangalore, Canada ,Chandan Nagar , Delhi, Deoria, Dhanbad, Durgapur, Gorakhpur , Jabalpur, Kodarma, Kolkata, Lucknow, Motihari, Mumbai ,Patna, Pune, Varanasi , Siwan, Bankura.
The Society has taken a target of 100 cities across 10 contries for next year.

My birthday - My Plant

Green Initiative Group of Hamara Sankalp celebrates society memebers'Birthday in unique style. In the Morning the Committee members visit the residence of the member on his/her Birthday and wish him/her Happy Birthday with Flowers. Later He/She is taken to the place of Plantation and a plant is planted by him/her and the Environment pledge is taken by Him/her with a promise to safe guide the plant for a year till next birthday. In the Evening a Photo Frame Momento is given to the member as His/Her Birthday Gift. This program is being coordinate by Shri Ajay Verma and Shri Umesh Prasad.

YOGA SADHANA

As it is said HEALTH IS THE BIGGEST WEALTH of us and to live a happy life we need to be physically as well as mentally healthy. In order to improve physical and mental health of Society members of their family Sports and Community Fitness group of Hamara Sankalp has been organizing Weekly YOG SADHANA program on Every Sunday at Burnpur Boy’s High School Premises under able guidance of Yoga Guru Dr. Prem Kant Jha of PATANJALI.
Members and their family members are availing this YOG Sadhana Program on regular basis and remarkable improvement in their health. Shri E Shamshi – CGM ISP and Shri S K Kushwaha Principal of BBHS helped the society t in getting the venue of Yog Sadhana.
Shri Rajesh Deogharia and Mrs Munmun Rai are coordinating this program and successfully organizing it every week.

Burnpur Gully Cricket 2020

16th February 2020 has become a memorable and unforgettable day for the Burnpur friends of 90s Group. On this day the Group organised a cricket tournament, The Gully Cricket- Edition 2.

Three teams participated in the tournament viz. Durgapur Devils, Rangapara Rebellions, and Burnpur Bangers. The tournament was played in round robin format with a new format called 50-50, it’s not 50 overs match but teams were given 50 balls each to play an inning. The new rule of 5 ball an over was introduced which was highly appreciated by all participants.
